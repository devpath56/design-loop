Harbor Notes

Shared reading for small teams

Every Friday, one member picks a chapter. The rest of the team reads it before the next Friday and leaves marginal notes — no scheduled call required.

Join a reading circle

There's no feed, no likes, no read receipts. Notes appear as marginalia next to the paragraph they respond to, the way they would in a shared physical copy.

Harbor Notes currently supports EPUB and plain text. PDF annotation is on the list, not yet built.
