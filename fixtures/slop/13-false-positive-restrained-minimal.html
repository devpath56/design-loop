A note posted February 2026

We are closing the storefront for March

The wheel room floods every spring when the snowmelt runs down the hill behind the studio, and this year we're finally doing the drainage work instead of mopping around it. That means the storefront will be closed for the month of March.

Online orders placed before February 28th will ship as usual. Anything placed after that date will go out the first week of April, once the floor is dry and the shelves are back where they belong.

If you're local and have a piece on the drying rack that needs picking up, come by before the 28th — after that the building will be hard to get into for a few weeks.

We'll post here again once the room is dry and the wheels are back in place. Thank you for being patient with a very old building.

— Dana, at the wheel
