Semi-transparent text over a striped background

This text uses rgba(0,0,0,0.4) as its color and sits over alternating black and white diagonal stripes. Wherever a glyph lands on a black stripe, the composited result is very dark text on a black surface -- effectively invisible. Wherever a glyph lands on a white stripe, the composited result is a mid-gray on white, which is marginal at best.
